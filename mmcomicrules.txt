RULES FOR MYLES COMICS!

Joke rules
1: Make sure the jokes have layers. Don't rely on one liners to make it funny.
2: Don't make any political/contraversial/racist jokes.

Art rules
1: Don't even think about using circle and line tools (An exception is using the line tool for lines between panels)
2: Stick to the art style. Make sure to draw them on model.
3: Make sure everything is readable and understandable.
4: Don't change character colors. Unless they are in a situation such as being splattered by red paint and what not.
5: Don't draw the characters in overthick outlines.
6: Font should be a comic font, or a Arial font. It should NOT be comic sans.
7: Don't draw the characters in MS Paint.

Other rules
1: Don't ship Myles and Drake, I have a feeling it would cause a lot of contraversy, and division in the fandom. A non-divided fandom is a happy fandom.
2: Don't insert OC's. Seems a bit off.
3: Don't put the characters in violent, political, or Not Safe for Work situations.
4: The minimum panel number is 3. The maximum us 6.
5: Don't insert anything from other franchises and games.
6: Don't put Pokemon trainers inside comic strips. Pokeballs are also not allowed

Character rules
1: Make sure the characters act how they would

- Myles should act slightly like a child, niave and adventurous, and sometimes build things that make weird situations (Not all the time, dont make it all about it)
- Drake is kind of a everyman of the group, less niave and adventurous but still a little.

If there are anymore characters, i might add them here

2: Make the characters act like how they would in a situation
